THE WORDS HE COULD'T SAY


This is the story of a guy in the form of poem specially about his heart
And how his heart bore the pain of his and his beloved drifting far apart
He didn’t have the courage to say that she was the silver lining to his dark cloud
And it’s not the silence that haunts him, but its the words that he could never speak out loud

He had a crush on her for a long time and guess in terms of love, he was pretty late
He wanted to say, the moon is beautiful, isn’t it, but was forced to say sunset
He tried so hard to get her and did so much efforts just to be with her
But he never showed it to her and due to which he always used to suffer

He always believed that efforts to matter so he decided to give a final throttle
It took some time but eventually he realized that he was just nursing an empty bottle
And that realization left him with immense pain, enduring scars and bruises
He can beat the world for her but can’t beat the man she chooses

He always thought about her and always loved her but she didn’t care
And some stories don’t have a happy ending rather they are destined to end in despair
He never gave up on her and she never gave up on him and in life both were quiet free
It would be an amazing story if it was of 2 persons but unfortunately it was of three
