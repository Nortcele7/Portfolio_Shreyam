EMPTY SPACES BETWEEN MEMORIES

Stepped outside in the gardens and saw a lightning jewel
Saw you in only nights, don’t know why God is cruel
Maybe you are only meant for the nights, to make them shine
And I whispered to myself that your presence makes the night divine

Never saw your back face and only front was enough
Since you will be gone, welcoming the day is always tough
Even you are not in day, the day seems pretty fine
But the night becomes more gorgeous because of your moonshine

Saying goodbye to the night is hardest because you will be gone
And staying up all day without you is like sitting on throne
We might not be united in day but will always be in night
And you are the only one who makes my life bright

You may have 8 different phases but I like them all
Even in new moon I don’t find you dull
Many people say that your phase as full moon is elegant
But in all of your 8 phases I find you excellent
