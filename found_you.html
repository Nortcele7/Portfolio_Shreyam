FOUND YOU


Entered the college gates and sat on my desk
And I saw somebody who was prettier than snow flakes
After seeing you my wretched heart became fortunate
After the moment I saw you, I always thanked my fate

21st August 2022 was the date to be exact
After that your memories made my mind stacked
I was yearning so much knowing you can’t be mine
But one thing I am sure that girl you are divine

I found you but you didn’t even noticed me
And I am sure that you have never ever missed me
But I really miss you girl every single day
“Make her mine” is always what I pray

Searched you through social media but you weren’t there
I am searching you all day and you don’t even care
