THE LOVE I COULDN'T UNFEEL


You will never know how much I loved you and how many times I fell for you
And you will never understand how much I wished that you felt the same I do
I was trying to be close to you but you were the one who was drifting away
Knowing you would never ever catch me, I kept falling for you anyway

You were my true and pure love that I expected to last for ages
And I wrote poems for you but you never turned the pages
I was just trying to make you mine because I loved you and that’s true
It hurts to know that you are happy without me in a way I could never be without you

Now, I am finally letting you go and I know now that from my life you will be gone
And letting go is not the hardest part rather it’s realizing that you never wanted to hold on
I wrote the words for you in the sand but it was constantly washing away by tide
And I learned the hard way that love is real even if it is only felt on one side

For me, you were the world, but for you, my presence was just like a drama show
And sometimes, the heart holds on to something that the mind knows it should let go
And I guess I can never unlove you and you also felt the same way for someone new
And it’s not the emptiness that hurts, what hurts is that emptiness is perfectly filled by you
