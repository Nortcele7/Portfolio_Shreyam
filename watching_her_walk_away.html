WATCHING HER WALK AWAY


He loved her purely and every time he sees her or even thinks about her his emotions go burst
But every time he wants to text her, he realizes that there is a guy in her life who doesn’t have to text first
He genuinely fell in love with her and wanted to spend the rest of his life with her
But she also loved someone new and his all of the efforts doesn’t even matter

When the infinite efforts that he did for her went in vain, he felt immense pain
He tried to forget this part of his life but circumstances made him remember again and again
This was the most beautiful chapter in his life but he did want to forget it
And now the only thing he ever wishes for, is that they never ever meet

He wanted to be with her and support her as well as stand for her when he was there
She was the girl of his fondest dreams but in her life, he was just like a nightmare
After realizing that she doesn’t have interest in him he got out of his mind and lost his track
Guess there’s a special kind of pain in waiting for someone who he knew is never coming back

He lost her in every universe and this is what we call A Life’s Merciless and Dreadful game
He didn’t regret loving her but regretted his hope thinking that she would feel the same
He loved her girl of dreams and so she did with her man of dreams and there is no one to blame
But the saddest part is watching someone you love, fall in love with someone else in the same way you fell for them
