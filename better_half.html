BETTER HALF


She is the one who holds my complete trust,
Her smile ignites my emotions with a burst
Her innocence brings endless laughter to me,
And I long to be with my perfect harmony.

She understands the sorrows I’ve endured,
She sees the passions with which I’m assured.
Yet she remains unaware that she is my dream,
And making her mine feels like an impossible scheme.

She isn’t present on any social media site,
Nowhere to be found, not even in Wikipedia's light.
She doesn’t realize how deeply I am drawn,
Her absence leaves me in sorrow from dusk till dawn.

Yet in my heart, a hope gently thrives,
That fate might align our parallel lives.
Perhaps someday, our paths will entwine,
And I’ll finally call her truly mine.

Until then, I cherish the dreams we share,
Hoping for a moment when she'll be aware.
For in my soul, she's my endless song,
My better half, where my heart belongs.
